Content

Home page

The main idea is that people entering my website can see two things since I am going to focus on two audiences, people interested in art and people interested in programming. The main page is very simple, with two squares with two options, one option takes people to my catalog of drawings that I will upload weekly, and the other option is programming, people can see my progress or code projects.

Also in the footer, my social networks with a direct link to my profile, an email, and the Linkedin link.

I want my web page to be able to visit the maximum number of people possible, that will be thanks to the effort I make on social networks as well. I want artists, programmers and also companies to see my talent and my passion.

Images for the Home page

Drawing

Page two, which would be the page where I would upload my drawings, would be an extensive catalog, next to each image the name of the work of art, the date, the type of art, and if it is digital or hand-painted. Under each illustration I am going to put a box where people can ask about the image, the message will arrive in my email, with the question and what image it is from, the idea is that I can interact with people a little closer. Under each image, people can rate with stars and also donate money. Also on page two, below everything, there will be an option for commissions (Drawings that people ask me to make for them). I will also make a small section of tips and tools that I use for my drawings, very similar to what I will do on Instagram and my social networks.

The main idea of page two is that people see my drawings and enjoy the art as I do, also the other objective, is that companies looking for programmers, see my site.

Images for the Page 2

Programing

The function of page three is to upload my personal projects or programming tasks, it is like a portfolio, divided into folders with the name of the project with simple images. Inside each folder people will have the option to download it and also a comment box that people can write and everyone will see a feedback.
